About PhishSnitch

Empowering users to recognise and respond to phishing attacks before they strike.

🔍 What is PhishSnitch?

PhishSnitch is an interactive cybersecurity education platform designed to enhance your awareness of phishing and social engineering attacks. Through scenario-based quizzes and bite-sized learning, we help you build a human firewall against the most common threats on the internet.

🎯 Our Mission

In a world where cyber threats evolve daily, our mission is to bridge the gap between technical defences and user awareness. We aim to make cybersecurity knowledge accessible, engaging, and actionable for everyone—from beginners to tech-savvy users.

🧠 How It Works

  • Pick a category (e.g., Email Phishing, Vishing, or Malware).
  • Take a quick quiz to test your instincts and learn from your choices.
  • Track your progress and revisit questions you missed.

🔒 Why It Matters

90% of cyber breaches begin with human error. PhishSnitch focuses on turning the average user into an aware one. Because in cybersecurity, knowledge is your best defence.
